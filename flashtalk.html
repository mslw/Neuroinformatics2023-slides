Michał Szczepanik
@doktorpanik 		Psychoinformatics group,
Institute of Neuroscience and Medicine, Brain & Behavior (INM-7),

Setup

  • Collaborative Research Center
  • Key Mechanisms of Motor Control in Health and Disease
  • 24 research groups: animal, human, clinical, & cross-linking

Challenge

  • Diverse data (origin, storage, privacy)
  • Shared data resource for computational modelling

Solution

  • Decentralized research data management
  • Projects control standards, storage, sharing
  • DataLad as overlay structure (indexing data at its location)

Software ecosystem

  • Version control & availability tracking
  • Data presentation & data portal
  • Metadata extraction
  • Graphical user interface
  • Manual dataset descriptions
  • Useful storage infrastructure
